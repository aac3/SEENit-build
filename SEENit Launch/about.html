About Us

Who We Are

SEENit is a site made by movie lovers for movie lovers. The site was designed with the idea to help movie lovers keep track of the movies they have seen. Over time keeping track of movies can become a boring chore and very time consuming. With SEENit, users can easily create list and add movies to that list. In addition, we wanted users to be able to share their list with friends, compete with friends, and write reviews. When it came to providing movie posters and movie information, we decided to use the powerful Rotten Tomato API. This gives user a large library of movies with all the important information. Pairing our site with Rotten Tomatoâ€™s API was a no brainer for us. We wanted to make sure our users got the best library possible.

SEENit is a website project created and designed by Arlan Craig. He created this site as his final project at Full Sail University. The site is still being overseen by him and will continue to be updated. This idea of the site was not just a project idea, but also an idea He wanted for himself.

We hope you enjoy the site. We will continue to provide users with new or better options and ways to keep track of their movies. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us. We ask that you follow us on Facebook and Twitter to keep up with latest news about the site. THANK YOU!
